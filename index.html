Bioinformatics & Data Science in Modern Healthcare

Modern healthcare systems generate enormous amounts of data every single day including genetic sequences, laboratory results, imaging reports, clinical notes, wearable device readings and electronic health records. Bioinformatics and data science transform this raw information into meaningful medical insights that support diagnosis, treatment and prevention.
