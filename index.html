Introduction

Bioinformatics and data science are interdisciplinary fields that combine biology, computer science, statistics, and artificial intelligence to analyze complex biological and medical data. In healthcare, these approaches help in understanding diseases, improving diagnosis, and developing effective treatment strategies.

The rapid growth of genomic data, electronic health records, and medical imaging has made data-driven decision-making an essential component of modern healthcare systems.

Major Applications in Healthcare

  • Genomics and Sequence Analysis: Identification of genetic variations associated with diseases.
  • Disease Prediction: Use of data science models to predict disease risk and progression.
  • Personalized Medicine: Customizing treatments based on individual genetic profiles.
  • Medical Imaging Analysis: Application of AI algorithms for accurate image-based diagnosis.
  • Clinical Decision Support Systems: Supporting healthcare professionals with data-driven insights.
